Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Primary author of the Declaration of Independence; later served as the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
Strong advocate for independence; became the 2nd President of the United States and played a key diplomatic role during the Revolution.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned inventor, writer, and diplomat; helped secure French support for the American Revolution.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress; known for his large and bold signature on the Declaration.
Samuel Adams
Revolutionary leader and founder of the Sons of Liberty; cousin of John Adams and instrumental in organizing colonial resistance.
Roger Sherman
The only person to sign all four major American founding documents; played a central role in shaping the U.S. Constitution.
Robert Morris
Financier of the American Revolution; helped fund the Continental Army and later served as Superintendent of Finance.

These men are remembered not only for signing the Declaration but also for their enduring contributions to the founding and shaping of the United States.
