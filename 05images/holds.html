Hold Types

You will find below indoor climbing holds. These will be easier to understand than outdoor holds as natural rock isn't as intuitive unless you're already familiar with hold types. There are several different kinds of holds. Hold types can also combine to make a new kind of hold. For example: a jug and a pocket are two different kinds of holds, but combined, you can have a juggy pocket. You will also see holds like a crimpy pinch, slopy jug, slopy pinch, etc. The possibilities are endless.
